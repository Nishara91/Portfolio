Hi, I'm Nishara Wickramarathna

Sub Inspector of Special Task Force

Hello, I am Nishara Wickramarathana, a dedicated Sub Inspector with the Special Task Force (STF) in the Sri Lanka Police. Born on July 26, 1991, I am currently 32 years old. My journey in the STF began in 2012, and I am a proud alumnus of Embilipitiya President College and D.S. Senanayaka College, Colombo.

During my school days, I excelled as a rugby player, representing both my school and the STF rugby team. I also displayed my talents in football and kabaddi, having been a school kabaddi player at Embilipitiya President College. My commitment to education led me to complete a six-month course at BOC, Embilipitiya, before joining the STF.

In my years of service, I have worked in various regions across Sri Lanka, gaining invaluable experience. From 2015 to 2022, I was part of the Search and Bomb Disposal Division, where I had the privilege of serving in VVIP duties, including former presidents and prime ministers, as well as parliamentary security. Additionally, I completed a regional response course in collaboration with the Sri Lanka Atomic Energy Board, contributing to the protection of radiation sources in our country.

In 2022, I embarked on a new academic journey, enrolling in a four-year degree program in BNs (Hons) Information Technology at Kotelawala Defence University, while continuing to follow my passion for law enforcement. I entered the bond of marriage in 2021 with my wife, Anupama Gunasekara, who is an English teacher.

My pursuit of knowledge extends beyond my law enforcement career. I hold high diplomas in Social Psychology Counseling from Sri Jayawardhanapura University and Distance Death Investigation (Forensic) from Colombo Campus. As I continue my degree program, I remain committed to my role in the STF and the enhancement of my skills.

This portfolio encapsulates my journey, reflecting my dedication, diverse experiences, and unwavering commitment to serving my nation and community.
